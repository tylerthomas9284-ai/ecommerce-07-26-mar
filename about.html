Our Story

Made by Hand.
Chosen with Care.

Lumora Home began with a simple frustration — it had become too hard to find home objects that were honestly made. Every beautiful thing we touched turned out to be factory-produced, rushed, or disposable. We started Lumora to change that, sourcing exclusively from small workshops and independent artisans whose work deserves to be in the homes of people who care.

What Drives Us

Our Values

Three ideas that guide every decision we make — from who we partner with to how we pack your order.

01

Honest Making

Every piece we carry is made by a real person using real skill. We visit the workshops, we know the makers, and we never sell anything we wouldn't keep in our own home.

02

Slow & Intentional

We don't chase trends. We look for objects with staying power — pieces that work in a room for decades, not just a season. Less but better has always been our edit philosophy.

03

Fair to the Earth

Natural materials, low-waste packaging, and artisans who harvest responsibly. We measure our footprint carefully and are always looking for ways to reduce it further.

From a Small Studio in San Francisco

We started in a single room in the Mission District in 2021 — a table, a laptop, and a growing list of artisans whose work we believed in. The first collection was twelve pieces. We shipped them all from our kitchen table.

Four years later, the collection has grown to over a hundred carefully chosen pieces, and we now work with artisan workshops across the US, Portugal, Vietnam, and Japan. Every new maker we add goes through the same process: we visit in person, we try the product ourselves for months, and only then do we offer it to you.

Lumora is still small by design. Small enough to know every product, every maker, and every customer who writes to us. That is not going to change.
