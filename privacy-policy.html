Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2025

Lumora Home (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or place an order with us.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Order and transaction details

We also collect non-personal information automatically, including browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our website and product offerings
  • Send promotional emails (only with your consent)

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You may decline cookies via the banner shown on your first visit, though some site features may be limited as a result. We do not use cookies for advertising tracking or share cookie data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With delivery partners to fulfill your order
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of Lumora Home or our customers

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@lumorahome.site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
